<h2>Top Attractions in Bangladesh</h2>
<p>When it comes to top attractions in Bangladesh, the list is endless. From UNESCO World Heritage Sites to hidden gems off the beaten path, there is something for every type of traveler to explore and discover in this diverse country.</p>
<p>One of the must-visit attractions in Bangladesh during the summer is the Sundarbans, the largest mangrove forest in the world and home to the majestic Royal Bengal tigers. Nature enthusiasts can take a boat tour through the winding waterways, spot exotic wildlife, and marvel at the breathtaking natural beauty of this unique ecosystem.</p>

<h2>Best Places to Visit</h2>
